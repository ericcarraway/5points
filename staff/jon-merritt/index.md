Jon Merritt currently teaches the Ronald Reagan High School Drumline and maintains a large studio of private students in the San Antonio area.

Prior to his move to Texas, Jon was a Band Director and Percussion Director of Hilliard Darby High School, where his groups were consistent WGI Scholastic World Finalists.

As a performer, Jon won a World Championship as the section leader of the 1993 Cadets. &nbsp;Jon is a graduate of Ohio University in Athens, Ohio.

In his spare time, Jon enjoys video games, riding dirt bikes, and judging indoor percussion all over the country.

Jon is a member of the Innovative Percussion Educational Team. -->
